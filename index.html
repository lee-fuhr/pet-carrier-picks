How we choose our picks

We build our recommendations by looking at three sources: long-term owner reviews from major retailers and pet forums, published expert evaluations from organizations like the Center for Pet Safety and WGACA, and common failure patterns (broken zippers, torn mesh, collapsing frames). We don’t run our own lab tests or timed trials; instead, we synthesize what hundreds of real users and reputable testers have found over time. Our picks emphasize reliability, airline compliance, and pet comfort. A carrier that scores well in all three categories but has a few complaints about zipper stiffness may still beat one that’s perfect in one area but fails in another. We also favor designs that are easy to clean and carry, because those features matter most in daily use.
